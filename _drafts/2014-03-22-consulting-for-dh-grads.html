Specific to web design/dev, but useful for any consulting job (e.g. research, editing)

Agree that emails/research for question-answering is reimbursed

Keep spreadsheet of hours worked/on what

Rate per hour, then estimate hours needed for different activities

Hold 40% for taxes (depends on state, total amount made in year over $600?, LLC or what status) and figure that into final quote

Clarity on finished point and whether can take emails after and what charge for any emails after

Emails before agreement can add up in costs of hours and research

State your availability to answer emails at any time

State availability of hours to work on project per week

In initial email, state preferred time period (e.g. negotiations can take so long you'll no longer be able to work by the time price is agreed on)
