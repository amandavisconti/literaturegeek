My essay on Grangerizing is up over at the Archbook online encyclopedia on the architectures of the book! Check it out here. There was more florid anti-Grangerizing invective than I could fit into the essay, so also check out my previous Grangerizing post for more!

Archbook is "is an open-access, peer-reviewed collection of richly illustrated essays about specific design features in the history of the book". Other entries include manicules (yay!), volvelles, and flaps, and they're looking for more entries on a variety of book-materiality topics.
